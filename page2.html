About Cogito, Ergo Sum

Cogito, Ergo Sum is a neural interfacing technology project that I am interested in developing. It is focused on creating technology that enables users to interact with digital systems through mere thought.

Core Objectives

  • Developing safe and accessible brain-computer interfaces that require no surgery and are removable at will.
  • Enhancing communication speed between users and technology
  • Revolutionizing accessibility for individuals with physical limitations
